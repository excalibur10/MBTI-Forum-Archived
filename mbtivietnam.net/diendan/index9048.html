    1. Given the choice of anyone in the world, whom would you want as a dinner guest?
    ai thiên về ăn mặn giống mình

    2. Would you like to be famous? In what way?
    k thích nổi tiếng nhưng nếu nó mang lại sự giàu có thì chấp nhận chịu áp lực

    3. Before making a telephone call, do you ever rehearse what you are going to say? Why?
    có list ra để khỏi nói thiếu

    4. What would constitute a “perfect” day for you?
    k bị tai nạn hay xui xẻo là perfect rồi

    5. If you were able to live to the age of 90 and retain either the mind or body of a 30-year-old for the last 60 years of your life, which would you want?
    k biết

    6. Do you have a secret hunch about how you will die?
    khi làm việc gì nguy hiểm quá nhiều lần thì sẽ nghĩ "sớm muộn gì cũng chết"

    7. For what in your life do you feel most grateful?
    sinh ra trong gia đình giàu có

    8. If you could change anything about the way you were raised, what would it be?
    ??

    9. If you could wake up tomorrow having gained any one quality or ability, what would it be?
    h chơi realist hay science fiction để biết đường trả lời

    10. If a crystal ball could tell you the truth about yourself, your life, the future or anything else, what would you want to know?
    where do the nearest aliens live?

    11. Is there something that you’ve dreamed of doing for a long time? Why haven’t you done it?
    chưa đủ tiền haha T.T

    12. What is the greatest accomplishment of your life?
    me. right now

    13. What do you value most in a friendship?
    ride or die

    14. What is your most treasured memory?

    15. What is your most terrible memory?

    16. If you knew that in one year you would die suddenly, would you change anything about the way you are now living? Why?
    về phụ giúp cha mẹ thay vì trên tp ích kỷ theo đuổi ước mơ

    17. What does friendship mean to you?
    support

    18. What roles do love and affection play in your life?
    ?

    19. How close and warm is your family? Do you feel your childhood was happier than most other people’s?
    too warm

    20. How do you feel about your relationship with your mother?
    hầu như mình toàn có duyên với những ai có bad relationship with mom
    nhưng mà những thằng đàn ông k có mối qhe tốt với mẹ k có nghĩa họ sẽ đối xử tệ với bạn gái mình, mấy bạn đồng ý k?

    21. When did you last cry in front of another person? By yourself?
    bị chọt đau nên khóc trước ngta

    22. What, if anything, is too serious to be joked about?
    chắc k có

    23. If you were to die this evening with no opportunity to communicate with anyone, what would you most regret not having told someone? Why haven’t you told them yet?
    mình muốn contact lại với một người lâu lắm rồi, hỏi họ đã chuyển vào tp chưa, ở đâu để mình qua chơi, ect. nói chung là muốn nc lại với ngta, be casual, mà sao khó quá, ng đó chủ động cắt trao đổi với mình trc vì lý do gì đó, h mình contact lại như làm phiền/làm hỏng "plan" của ngta

    24. Your house, containing everything you own, catches fire. After saving your loved ones and pets, you have time to safely make a final dash to save any one item. What would it be? Why?
    grab lap

    25. Of all the people in your family, whose death would you find most disturbing? Why?
    anh trai, vì thân nhất
